## Overview

Rad Survival Guide is an educational study app designed to help users prepare for radiography-related registry study through study cards, practice questions, progress tracking, reminders, simulated positioning practice, tutorial screens, and related study tools.

This Privacy Policy explains what information Rad Survival Guide stores, uses, and shares.

Rad Survival Guide is an independent study tool and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by ARRT.

## Information Stored on Your Device

Rad Survival Guide stores certain information locally on your device so the app can track your study progress, personalize your experience, and support app features.

This may include:

* Study progress
* Flagged study cards
* Focus deck cards
* Flagged practice questions
* Subsection performance statistics
* Missed question history
* Past practice test results
* Completed practice test count
* Trophy and achievement progress
* Notification and reminder settings
* Exam date or exam month settings
* App theme or onboarding settings
* Beta access status and redemption date
* App access settings used for local app behavior
* Positioning Lab tutorial completion status
* Locally stored issue or bug report entries, if you choose to use those features

This information is stored locally on your device using app storage. Rad Survival Guide does not currently sync this information to a Chasing Lycan Studios server.

If you delete the app or clear app data, locally stored progress and settings may be deleted.

## Positioning Lab and Simulated Imaging Practice

Rad Survival Guide may include interactive radiography positioning practice tools, including simulated patient positioning, central ray placement, collimation practice, tutorial screens, and projection-based educational feedback.

These features use in-app educational graphics and simulated practice scenes.

Rad Survival Guide does not collect or upload real patient images, x-ray images, medical images, camera images, protected health information, clinical records, microphone audio, location data, contacts, or user photos through the Positioning Lab.

Positioning Lab activity is intended for educational study and practice only. It should not be used as medical, clinical, or professional advice.

## Purchases and Subscription Information

Rad Survival Guide uses RevenueCat and app store billing systems, such as Google Play Billing where applicable, to process and manage purchases, subscriptions, restorations, and entitlement status.

Rad Survival Guide may offer:

* Monthly Full Access subscription
* Lifetime Full Access one-time purchase
* Restore Purchase functionality

RevenueCat and app store billing systems may process purchase-related information such as purchase status, subscription status, transaction information, app identifiers, device identifiers, and entitlement status.

RevenueCat’s Google Play Data Safety guidance states that apps using RevenueCat should disclose purchase history as collected by RevenueCat.

Rad Survival Guide uses RevenueCat CustomerInfo and entitlement status to determine whether Full Access is active. The app does not permanently store a “paid forever” flag for subscriptions. If RevenueCat no longer reports an active entitlement, subscription-based Full Access may end unless another access source, such as Lifetime Full Access or active beta access, applies.

For more information about RevenueCat’s privacy practices, please review RevenueCat’s Privacy Policy.

## Beta Access Codes

Rad Survival Guide may provide beta access codes or other access codes to certain testers or users.

Access code status may be stored locally on your device and may unlock Full Access for a limited period or according to the rules of that access code. The current access code system is local to the app and does not create an account or user profile with Chasing Lycan Studios.

Access codes may expire after the applicable access period or may be limited by the rules shown or communicated for that code.

These may include:
