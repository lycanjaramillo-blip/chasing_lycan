Privacy Policy

1. Overview

Rad Survival Guide is an educational study app designed to help users prepare for radiography-related registry study through study cards, practice questions, progress tracking, reminders, and related study tools.

This Privacy Policy explains what information Rad Survival Guide stores, uses, and shares.

Rad Survival Guide is an independent study tool and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by ARRT.

2. Information Stored on Your Device

Rad Survival Guide stores certain information locally on your device so the app can track your study progress and personalize your experience.

This may include:

study progress
flagged study cards
focus deck cards
flagged practice questions
subsection performance statistics
missed question history
past practice test results
completed practice test count
trophy and achievement progress
notification/reminder settings
exam date or exam month settings
beta access status and redemption date
app access settings used for local app behavior

This information is stored locally on your device using app storage. Rad Survival Guide does not currently sync this information to a Chasing Lycan Studios server.

If you delete the app or clear app data, locally stored progress may be deleted.

3. Purchases and Subscription Information

Rad Survival Guide uses RevenueCat and Google Play to process and manage purchases, subscriptions, restorations, and entitlement status.

Rad Survival Guide may offer:

Monthly Full Access subscription
Lifetime Full Access one-time purchase
Restore Purchase functionality

RevenueCat and Google Play may process purchase-related information such as purchase status, subscription status, transaction information, app identifiers, device identifiers, and entitlement status. RevenueCat’s Google Play Data Safety guidance states that apps using RevenueCat should disclose purchase history as collected by RevenueCat.

Rad Survival Guide uses RevenueCat CustomerInfo and entitlement status to determine whether Full Access is active. The app does not permanently store a “paid forever” flag for subscriptions. If RevenueCat no longer reports an active entitlement, subscription-based Full Access may end unless another access source, such as Lifetime Full Access or active beta access, applies.

For more information about RevenueCat’s privacy practices, see RevenueCat’s Privacy Policy.

4. Beta Access Codes

Rad Survival Guide may provide beta access codes to certain testers or users.

Beta access codes are stored locally on your device and may unlock Full Access for a limited period. The current beta access system is local to the app and does not create an account or user profile with Chasing Lycan Studios.

Beta access may expire after the applicable access period.

5. Support and Issue Reports

Rad Survival Guide includes a Contact Support / Report Issue feature.

When you choose to send a support message, the app opens your email client using a prefilled email addressed to:

lycanjaramillo@gmail.com

The email may include:

the message you typed
optional contact information you entered
your current plan/access status
platform information
build mode
date and time

The app does not send support messages silently. You can review and edit the email before sending it through your email app.

6. Notifications and Reminders

Rad Survival Guide may use local notifications for study reminders and exam-related reminders.

These may include:

inactivity reminders
weekly exam countdown reminders
3-day exam reminders
day-of-exam reminders
post-exam re-engagement reminders

These notifications are scheduled locally on your device. Rad Survival Guide does not currently use a remote push notification server.

You can turn study reminders off in the app settings.

7. Information Not Currently Collected by Chasing Lycan Studios

Rad Survival Guide does not currently include:

account creation
username or password login
cloud sync
app-owned backend account storage
analytics SDK
crash reporting SDK
advertising SDK
silent support-report uploads

If these features are added in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated.

8. Third-Party Services

Rad Survival Guide uses third-party services and packages to provide app functionality.

These may include:

RevenueCat for purchase and entitlement management
Google Play for Android billing and subscription management
url_launcher to open email links
flutter_local_notifications for local reminders
shared_preferences for local app storage

Google Play requires developers to provide Data Safety information describing whether and how apps collect, share, and protect user data.

9. Managing Purchases and Subscriptions

Subscriptions and purchases are managed through Google Play and/or RevenueCat.

If you purchased through Google Play, cancellation and subscription management are handled through your Google Play account.

Canceling a subscription may stop future renewal, but access may continue until the end of the active billing or trial period.

10. Data Deletion

Because Rad Survival Guide currently stores study progress locally on your device, you can delete local app data by:

deleting the app from your device, or
clearing the app’s storage/data through your device settings

For support-related emails you send directly, you may contact:

lycanjaramillo@gmail.com

to request deletion of your support correspondence where reasonably possible.

Purchase and subscription records handled by Google Play or RevenueCat may be subject to those providers’ own retention and account policies.

11. Children’s Privacy

Rad Survival Guide is intended for students and users preparing for radiography-related study and is not directed toward young children.

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through a support email, please contact:

lycanjaramillo@gmail.com
